Lassagna

This is a very variety of flavors on a preparation, this recipe of lassagna also called: " pasticho" have an exra ingredient that make it more appetitive

We're making it with lamine of pasta, a variety of chessee, eggplant and bechamel dip! Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 Tomattoes
  • 2 Eggplants
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Pepper
  • 1 Garlic
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Suggar
  • Olive oil
  • 300gr of cutted meat
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Yello cheesse
  • 16 Lasagna noddles
  • 2 Cups of milk
  • 4 Spoons of pulverized cornstarch
  • Butter
  • Ham

Step by Step

  1. Slice the eggplants and soak for 30 min, chop the onion, garlic and tomattoes
  2. drain the eggplants and fry it. crumble some cheesse
  3. Cooked the eggplants make a layer of it on a plate
  4. When all of ingredients are done, We cook the pasta and remove from heat
  5. For the bechamel dip, in a blender add 2 cups of milk, 4 spoons of cornstarch, blend it while on a pan put some butter and when it'is melt we add bit a bit on the blender, add some salt.
  6. Now, we're making some layers of our ingredients, we add pasta, with some ketchup and mayo, add some eggplants, add sauces, pasta, ham, sauces, pasta, eggplants, chesses, pasta, at the end yellow chesse and sauces. go in the furnace at 200° for 20 min and VOILA!

Korean Corndogs

This dish, reinvented on the streets of Korea, has steadily grown in popularity over time; it features various fillings—ranging from mozzarella and sausage to other flavors—encased in a batter that is fluffy on the inside and crispy on the outside. The toppings vary widely, appealing to the consumer's sense of curiosity.

Many vendors offer a wide variety of condiments, flavors, and accompaniments for this dish; in the following recipe, we will look at a classic version.

Ingredients

  • ChopSticks
  • Mozzarella and sausages
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Warm water
  • Cooking oil

Step by Step

  1. Activate the yeast with some water, sugar and flour
  2. In a bowl mix flour, salt, sugar and season to taste with some spices blends
  3. Once the yeast is ready, gradually add water and an egg to the flour mix, until you have a somewhat pasty mixure, and let it rest for 30 min or like 1hr
  4. While the mix is resting, prepare some sausages and mozzarella on the chopsticks
  5. when the dough has doubled in size, enroll the chopstick with our core ingredient and be sure that everithing is covered by the dough.
  6. We deep-fry at a temperature of 170 degrees.
  7. Feel fre to decore with sauces, suggar or another condiment! Enjoy
